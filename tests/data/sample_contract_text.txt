MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2026
("Effective Date") by and between:

ACME Services LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Vendor")
and
BlueCo Inc., a California corporation ("Client")

ARTICLE 1: SERVICES AND RATES

1.1 Consulting Services
Vendor shall provide consulting services to Client at the following rates:
- Senior Consultant: Two Hundred US Dollars ($200.00) per hour
- Junior Consultant: One Hundred Twenty-Five US Dollars ($125.00) per hour
- Technical Specialist: One Hundred Seventy-Five US Dollars ($175.00) per hour

1.2 Project Management
Project management services shall be billed at a fixed rate of One Hundred Fifty
Dollars ($150.00) per hour.

ARTICLE 2: MILESTONES AND DELIVERABLES

2.1 Phase 1: Discovery and Planning
Upon completion and Client acceptance of Phase 1 deliverables, Client shall pay
Vendor a milestone payment of Twenty Thousand US Dollars ($20,000.00).

2.2 Phase 2: Implementation
Upon completion and Client acceptance of Phase 2 deliverables, Client shall pay
Vendor a milestone payment of Thirty-Five Thousand US Dollars ($35,000.00).

2.3 Phase 3: Go-Live Support
Upon completion of go-live activities and transition to steady-state operations,
Client shall pay Vendor a milestone payment of Fifteen Thousand US Dollars ($15,000.00).

ARTICLE 3: PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Invoice Schedule
Vendor shall invoice Client monthly for time and materials work performed in
the prior month, and upon achievement of milestones.

3.2 Payment Due
Payment terms are Net 30 days from the date of invoice.

3.3 Late Payment
Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month.

ARTICLE 4: EXPENSES

4.1 Reimbursable Expenses
Client shall reimburse Vendor for pre-approved travel and out-of-pocket expenses
at cost plus a 10% administrative fee.

ARTICLE 5: TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period
of twelve (12) months unless earlier terminated.

5.2 Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
